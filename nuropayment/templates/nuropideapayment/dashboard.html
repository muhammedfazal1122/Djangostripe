Installation Setup

  1. Log in to your WordPress admin console.
  2. Go to Plugins marketplace, then select Add New.
  3. Search for "Nuropedia" , then click on "Install Now".
  4. If you don't find the plugin in search, download it from this URL: https://nuropedia.nuronics.com
  5. Once downloaded, select "Upload Plugin" and choose the downloaded file.
  6. Click on "Installed Plugins" under Plugins, and activate Nuropedia.

SignUp and Activation

  1. Create account via signup page - https://nuropedia.nuronics.com/register
  2. Fill the details and complete the email verification for registering email address
  3. *Link your Google Calendar; Authorize Nuropedia to manage calendar events by activating the link. Check an email with subject line “Link your Calendar..”
  4. After Successful account creation and Calendar Integration, continue to Plan selection
  5. To Select Plan, navigate to https://nuropedia.nuronics.com/billing/ page

Plan selection & billing

  1. Go to https://nuropedia.nuronics.com/billing/ page
  2. Choose a plan
  3. Fill the details for payment confirmation
  4. Once confirmed, Go back to WordPress - CMS console and hit “Refresh”
  5. Access “Nuropedia” Section and click “Activate”
  6. Within 5 minutes, Nuropedia is ready to use on website.
